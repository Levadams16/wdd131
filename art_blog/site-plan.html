Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this assignment is to make a blog post where I can post stories that I have and artists can post their art to this blog and then share their art with each other.

Audience

My audience is mainly for people who like to make art and want to show off their art.

Dynamic elements

JavaScript will be able to make this webpage more dynamic by having the description be counting how many words there are in the box. I can also make it more dynamic so that the genre can have different text pop up depending on the genre chosen. For instance, if the genre news was chosen, it can show text saying, "Make sure the news is accurate!"

Content

Home page

Content of the home page will have a heading saying, "Create your story here" and it will have text boxes below it. One of which will be the Title. Below that will have a dropdown button with selected categories of posts to choose. Below that will have a big description input box that will have a word counter below that. Below that it will have a button that says, "Post Story". Below the button will have a line going across the page, then below that will have text saying "Saved Stories" which will have links to saved stories below it with the title of the pages being the links. Then, below that will have the footer which will have two buttons, one for going back to the top of the page, and the other to go to the posting page, which is the page they are on.

[Page 2]

Content of the child page below the navbar will have the title as the header of the page. Below that to the left, will have the genres chosen by the author. Below that in the middle of the screen will have the description with any images in the text that the author wants as well. Below that will have the same footer that is on the home page.
